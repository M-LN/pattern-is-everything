The Mission

Pattern is Everything is a free, open-source educational platform — 252 interactive topics across Machine Learning, Poetry, and Markets. No ads. No paywalls. No tracking cookies. Just patterns, made visual.

We believe learning should be accessible to everyone. Every diagram, formula, and code snippet on this site will always be free.

100%
of donations go to mental health

Every contribution you make goes directly to mental health organizations. We keep nothing. The site runs on zero cost — no servers to pay for, no team to fund. This is a passion project, and your generosity goes where it matters.

Where It Goes

Donations are directed to organizations working on mental health support and awareness. We believe that understanding patterns — in data, in language, in markets — starts with understanding ourselves.

Current recipient: To Write Love on Her Arms (TWLOHA) — a nonprofit dedicated to presenting hope and finding help for people struggling with depression, addiction, self-injury, and suicide.
